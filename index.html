About

I'm Max Kofsky, a data analyst with a passion for uncovering insights and driving business growth in the oil and gas industry here in Calgary, Alberta. With over 3 years of experience in the field, I bring a proven track record of delivering high-quality data analysis and reporting solutions to my clients.


My expertise lies in programming with the R language, particularly in developing custom data visualizations and interactive reports using Power BI. I have a strong foundation in SQL, allowing me to extract and manipulate data from various sources with ease. And, I'm skilled in exploratory data analysis, ensuring that I can identify key trends and patterns within the data to provide valuable insights to my clients.


In my current role at Revisionz, I work closely with cross-functional teams to collect, analyze, and visualize large datasets to inform strategic business decisions for the Suncor Asset Data Lifecycle Program. My analytical skills have helped my team identify potential risks and opportunities, optimize resources, and improve overall performance. I'm constantly seeking out new data sources and techniques to incorporate into my analyses, ensuring that my clients receive the most comprehensive and accurate insights possible.


I hold a Bachelor of Commerce degree with a concentration in Business Analytics from the University of Calgary, where I honed my skills in programming languages, algorithms, and data structures. Additionally, I completed coursework in statistics, data modeling, and data visualization to further develop my expertise. I continue to expand my knowledge through online courses and professional certifications to stay up-to-date with the latest industry trends and tools.
